Green Hell VR: Quest Edition is a fresh and refurbished look on the succesful PC title Green Hell but on the standalone Meta Quest VR headset! Delve into the jungle with realistic crafting, beautiful landscapes and a lot of dangers lurking in the thick bushes of the Amazonian jungle. You will need to learn and take notes of real-life survival techniques in order to survive these harsh circumstances.

Follow the story of Jake Higgins , a famous anthropologist, who is thrown deep into the emerald and impenetrable Amazonian rain forest - the green hell . What you discover will be worse than what you fought so hard against to survive.

Find yourself stranded in a dangerous jungle , surrounded by predators with only a trusty smartwatch, backpack, and the survival guide, so craft away! VR makes crafting even more realistic as you need to find the right elements and put them together in a specific way to make tools, spears, and shelter - all by hand! Watch your vitals and discover herbs with their medical potential to stay healthy.

Hunt wild animals to secure the next meal and defend yourself with a bow and arrow - work on your aim and level up your skill. With mechanics adjusted to virtual reality, Green Hell VR: Quest Edition is a truly immersive and demanding game - Find out how long you can stay alive.

This custom-made game for the Meta Quest 2 is made to take advantage of the maximum of performances the standalone headset can deliver. We used a plethora of cutting-edge technologies when it comes to Quest game development. ASW technology allows to provide the best experience of Green Hell VR: Quest Edition possible.

Bow & Arrows

Hunt wild animals to secure the next meal and defend yourself. This feature is fully immersive, which means that you need to work on your aim, level up your skill, and remember… always shoot for the head!

The Smartwatch

Top notch, solar powered smartwatch that gives you information about your body - macroelements, water or level of fatigue. Way more than the mundane one can provide, right?

The Notebook

No, not the movie! You are provided with a notebook where all your discoveries are printed for the future reference. There are some tips written in there, too! You will learn how to make a fire, build your camp, and what heals specific wounds and diseases.

Rod & Bait

Sounds easy, right? Well, you need to find the right bait for the fish you are trying to catch and of course make the rod. Be careful walking into water though - something might leech on to you.

Green Hell VR is an Open World Survival game set in the Amazon rainforest, based on the successful PC title Green Hell . You are left alone in the jungle with no food or equipment. To survive, you must learn real-life survival techniques , and crafting weapons can be a matter of life and death.

You will have to face the vagaries of the weather to make your way through through this mysterious forest and get out of this place in one piece . You will have to face your weaknesses, as well as hunger, thirst, fatigue, wild animals, tropical diseases and deteriorating mental health.

In the core of the colorful Amazonian rainforest , you will need to learn how this deadly environment work to be able to make your way through the forest and discover mysterious secrets.

In this realistic forest you will be able to find extremely varied animals, from the little capybaras up to the legendary jaguars. It is YOUR time to decide - click the button below if you are brave enough to survive in the Amazonian jungle ALL ON YOUR OWN!

Wishlist on Steam VR

Crafting

You are stranded in the Amazon with almost nothing, so crafting is your best friend. The VR experience makes crafting even more realistic as you need to find the right elements and put them together in a specific way to make tools or shelter.

Food sources

You don’t have to kill animals to survive. There are tasty and nutritious fruits like coconuts or bananas, a lot of mushrooms and plenty of herbs, bulbs and nuts. These are sources of healthy proteins, carbohydrates and fats.

Healing

Experiment with different potions to survive in the wild . You will discover herbs and their medical potential by mixing them, and making a brew over the fire. Be careful though, some herbs don’t go together, and some… well, you’ll see.
